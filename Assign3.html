Albert Einstein

Albert Einstein was a German-born theoretical physicist who developed the theory of relativity, which revolutionized our understanding of space, time, and gravity. He is best known for his mass-energy equivalence formula, E=mcÂ², which has been described as "the world's most famous equation."

Year Invention Description
1905 Special Theory of Relativity Einstein's first theory of relativity, which revolutionized our understanding of space and time.
